Aircraft sales and acquisition

Buying or selling an aircraft is a complex process, and you need to seek professional advice to ensure that nothing is overlooked. This, combined with experienced legal counsel, will ensure a smooth transaction. Wilfort Aero combines expertise in all segments of the business aviation industry to deliver a turnkey solution - from pre-purchase inspections to pricing negotiations, legal support, import or export requirements, aircraft registry selection, crew selection and appointment to maintenance and even refurbishment management.
